Introduction

As urbanization progresses at an unprecedented rate, cities are grappling with the multifaceted challenges of managing ever-growing transportation demands. Existing transportation infrastructures are increasingly inadequate, leading to common problems such as traffic congestion, inefficiencies in route management, and lack of scalable solutions. The need for innovative approaches to optimize urban transportation systems is more pressing than ever. In recent years, technological advances have opened new avenues for addressing these challenges. Blockchain technology, renowned for its ability to maintain secure, transparent, and decentralized records, offers substantial promise for enhancing data management within transportation systems. Concurrently, swarm intelligence, inspired by the collective behavior of social insects, offers a paradigm for devising adaptive and scalable problem-solving strategies in complex dynamic environments. This paper introduces a pioneering framework that synergizes the decentralization capabilities of blockchain with the adaptive problem-solving properties of swarm intelligence to enhance urban transportation networks. By employing blockchain, we ensure that transportation data remain untampered and accessible, fostering transparency and trust among stakeholders. Swarm intelligence complements this by enabling real-time, adaptive decision-making that dynamically regroups resources to alleviate congestion and optimize route efficiency. Our research is grounded in an extensive review of contemporary literature that explores individual applications of blockchain and swarm intelligence across various domains, including distributed computing, smart cities, and traffic management. However, the synthesis of these technologies in a singular application for urban transportation remains largely unexplored. By conducting comprehensive simulations, we demonstrate that our framework can significantly improve urban mobility, reducing congestion levels and enhancing overall traffic flow efficiency. This study not only contributes to the theoretical knowledge base but also holds practical implications for policymakers and urban planners. By providing a technologically viable solution to persistent transportation issues, our framework paves the way for smarter, more resilient cities capable of meeting the demands of future populations. As such, this paper endeavors to serve as a cornerstone for subsequent research that further amalgamates emerging computational technologies to solve intricate urban challenges.
